## Dental Lab Deep Workflows

- `shade-material-review` - Shade & Material Review: Shade match, material selection, prescription constraints, and remake risk.
- `scan-file-intake` - Scan File Intake: Digital scan files, prescription attachments, margin notes, and missing item checks.
- `remake-risk-review` - Remake Risk Review: Case history, fit issues, clinician notes, material failures, and prevention actions.
- `clinician-lab-messages` - Clinician Lab Messages: Case questions, lab updates, approval requests, and delivery communication.
